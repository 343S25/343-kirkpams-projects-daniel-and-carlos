Help Page

Thank you for using our Tier List Website! Below you will find a step by step guide to using our website.

  1. Press the "New Tierlist" button in the Nav bar to begin constructing your list.
  2. If you wish to make a new row or box, press the button "Add New Tier" or "Add Box". You can delete the row and it's boxes by clicking the delete button next to.
  3. To edit the content the box displays, simply click inside of the box and type what you want
  4. You can also change the color/position of the row by clicking on the color selector and by clicking the move up/down buttons.
  5. You can search up youtube videos by scrolling down past the box bar and clicking in the "Search Youtube Videos" bar.
  6. [NOT FINISHED] GIPHY API implementation, similar to youtube above.
  7. The boxes, videos and gifs can all be dragged and dropped into the rows, allowing you to rank them however you wish!
